Bank Feeds

  • What is the issue you’re experiencing with your connected bank account?
  • Did you get an Error message?
  • What is the error message?
  • Can you read the error pop-up message to me?
  • What is the name of the bank you are using?

Billing Charges

  • How much is the charge?
  • When was the charge posted?
  • Where was it charged? (Credit card, debit card, or checking account)?
  • Is the charge recurring?
  • What is the description of the charge?

Installation - Multiuser

  • How many workstations are you installing on?
  • Do you have a network access admin?
  • Which workstation will host the company file?
  • What version year of QuickBooks do you want to install?
  • How familiar are you with network installation processes?
  • Do you have time to see the installation through to the end?
  • Are all users going to be connected to the same network?

Installation - Single User

  • Ask probing and clarifying questions to determine basic information about the customer's goal.
  • Determine the customer's installation situation and needs: For example, what version and year of QuickBooks is the customer installing, and how many workstations will be installed?
  • Determine if the customer has a One Intuit Account setup and locate this account in Salesforce.
  • Determine if the product the customer purchased is linked to that One Intuit account in Salesforce.
  • Determine the best installation method based on the customer's circumstances.
  • Ensure the customer has Admin rights and the time needed to complete the installation process.
  • Confirm that the customer has downloaded QuickBooks Desktop or Desktop Manager if needed and saved the files somewhere they can easily find them (like their Windows desktop).
  • Make sure that the customer has their product and license number handy.
  • This information is provided to the customer when they purchase the software.
  • Verify that the customer's computer meets the system requirements.

Login Issues

  • Where exactly are you trying to log in?
  • What is the exact website you are trying to log into?
  • Where exactly in the product are you getting the prompt to log in?
  • What is happening when you try to log in?

Online Banking Issues

  • What Financial Institution are they using?
  • Has it ever worked before?
  • When was the last time it worked?
  • Set up the account in a new data file to see if the error persists.
  • This step is important to see if the problem is the data file or the bank.
  • If it is the bank, have we had the customer call the bank yet?
  • Are they using Direct Connect or Web Connect?
  • If Direct Connect, does the issue work if you download and import a Web Connect file? (Direct Connect is US-only feature)
  • Can they manually log in to the bank web site outside of QuickBooks?
  • Have you tried toggling the banking mode? (applies to US only)
